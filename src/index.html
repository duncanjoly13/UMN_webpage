I joined the Networking Research Group during my second semester as an undergraduate student under the mentorship of Jason Carpenter. I am broadly interested
in mobile network security and availability, Internet access equality, and privacy in computer networks.

I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Asian and Middle Eastern Studies at the University, and will graduate in Spring 2026.

Research

At the beginning of my second year as an undergraduate, I published Examining Interplay of Compression and Encryption and Applicability to 5G Teleoperations with
co-authors Jason Carpenter and Prof. Zhi-Li Zhang. I am currently assisting research efforts with Rostand A. K. Fezeu, Lilian Coelho de Freitas, Faaiq Bilal, and others.
